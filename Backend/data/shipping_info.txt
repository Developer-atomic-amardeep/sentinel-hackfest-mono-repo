WEIGHT-BASED ADDITIONAL FEES
-----------------------------
For packages exceeding standard weight limits, additional fees apply:

- Standard Weight: Up to 5 lbs (included in base shipping cost)
- Medium Weight: 5-15 lbs (add $3.99)
- Heavy Weight: 15-30 lbs (add $8.99)
- Extra Heavy: 30-50 lbs (add $15.99 - UPS Ground used)
- Oversized: Over 50 lbs (custom quote - contact customer service)

Examples by Product Category:
• Electronics (Laptops, Smartphones): Typically 3-6 lbs (standard or medium)
• Clothing (T-shirts, Jeans): Typically 6-12 oz per item (standard)
• Home & Kitchen (Blenders, Coffee Makers): Typically 5-10 lbs (medium)
• Cookware Sets: Typically 15-20 lbs (heavy weight, UPS Ground)
• Accessories (Phone Cases, Cables): Typically under 1 lb (standard)

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING NOTES
-----------------------------

1. CUSTOMS DUTIES & TAXES
   - Import duties, taxes, and customs fees are the responsibility of the customer
   - These fees are NOT included in your order total or shipping cost
   - Fees are collected by the carrier upon delivery or by customs
   - Amounts vary by country and product value
   - Contact your country's customs office for estimated fees

2. BATTERY RESTRICTIONS (ELECTRONICS)
   - Lithium-ion battery regulations apply to: Laptops, Smartphones, Tablets,
     Smartwatches, Wireless Earbuds, and Portable Chargers
   - Some restrictions may apply to certain countries
   - FedEx may require additional documentation for high-capacity batteries
   - Processing time for electronics with batteries: Add 1-2 business days

3. PRODUCT AVAILABILITY
   - Some products may not be available for international shipping due to
     regional restrictions or manufacturer guidelines
   - Check product page for international shipping eligibility
   - Contact customer support if you have questions

4. RETURNS
   - International returns are accepted within 30 days
   - Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
   - Refunds do not include original shipping charges or customs fees paid
   - See our Return Policy for complete details

SPECIAL SHIPPING SITUATIONS
================================================================================

SIGNATURE REQUIRED
- All orders over $500
- All overnight shipping orders
- International express shipments
- Someone must be present to sign for the package

DELIVERY ATTEMPTS
- Carriers will attempt delivery 3 times
- After 3 failed attempts, package returns to our warehouse
- Redelivery fees may apply ($10.99)
- You can request to pick up from carrier facility

LOST OR DAMAGED PACKAGES
- If tracking shows "Delivered" but you didn't receive it:
  * Check with neighbors, building manager, or household members
  * Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked delivered early)
  * Contact us within 48 hours: support@techmartexpress.com
  
- If package arrives damaged:
  * Take photos of box and contents immediately
  * Do not discard packaging
  * Contact us within 48 hours for replacement or refund
  * Keep all items and packaging for carrier inspection if needed

SHIPPING DELAYS
- Weather conditions, natural disasters, or carrier delays may affect delivery
- We'll notify you of any significant delays
- Check tracking for most up-to-date information

HOLIDAYS & PEAK SEASONS
- Expect 1-3 additional days during:
  * Thanksgiving week
  * December holiday season (Black Friday through New Year)
  * Major sales events
- Order early to ensure delivery by specific dates

MULTIPLE ITEMS & SPLIT SHIPMENTS
================================================================================

COMBINED SHIPMENTS
- We try to ship all items in your order together
- This minimizes shipping costs and environmental impact

SPLIT SHIPMENTS (When it happens)
- Items ship from different warehouses (electronics vs. clothing)
- Backordered or pre-order items
- Large/heavy items that require special handling

If your order is split:
- You'll receive separate tracking numbers for each shipment
- Shipping charges remain the same (not charged per package)
- All items typically arrive within 1-2 days of each other
