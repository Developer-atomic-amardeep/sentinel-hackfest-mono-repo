## Overview

At TechMart Express, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with an item, we offer a **30-day return policy** for most products. Please review this policy carefully to understand your options.

## 30-Day Return Window

### Standard Return Period
- You have **30 days from the date of delivery** to initiate a return
- Items must meet our return conditions (see category-specific requirements below)
- Return shipping label can be requested through your online account or customer support

### How to Calculate Your Return Window
- **Day 1** = The day your order is delivered (shown in tracking as "Delivered")
- **Day 30** = Last day to initiate return request
- Example: Order delivered March 1st → Return must be requested by March 31st

✓ Factory reset completed
✓ No physical damage or excessive wear
✓ IMEI/Serial number matches original product
✓ Activation locks removed (for smartphones, tablets, smartwatches)

## How to Return an Item

### Step-by-Step Return Process

#### Step 1: Initiate Return (Online or Phone)

**Option A: Online (Fastest)**
1. Log into your account at www.techmartexpress.com
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select the order containing the item to return
4. Click "Return Item" and select reason
5. Choose refund or exchange
6. Print your return label (if purchased)

**Option B: Phone Support**
1. Call 1-800-TECHMART (1-800-832-4627)
2. Have your order number ready
3. Explain reason for return
4. We'll email you a return authorization and label

#### Step 2: Package Your Return
- Use original packaging when possible
- Include ALL accessories, manuals, and components
- Remove any personal information from devices
- Secure packaging with tape (no loose items)
- Attach return label to outside of box

**Packing Tips:**
- Electronics: Wrap in bubble wrap or original foam inserts
- Clothing: Place in sealed plastic bag to protect from weather
- Fragile items: Use adequate padding and mark "FRAGILE"

#### Step 3: Ship Your Return
- Drop off at carrier location (USPS, FedEx, or UPS)
- Keep your tracking receipt until refund is processed
- Track your return shipment online

#### Step 4: Return Processing
- We receive and inspect your return (2-3 business days)
- Refund processed if item meets return conditions (1-2 business days)
- You receive refund confirmation email

## Refund Processing

### Refund Timeline

**Total Time: 5-7 Business Days (After We Receive Your Return)**

1. **Return Arrives:** Our warehouse receives your package
2. **Inspection:** 2-3 business days to inspect and verify condition
3. **Refund Issued:** 1-2 business days to process refund to original payment method

## Special Return Situations

### Lost or Stolen Packages

If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you didn't receive it:
1. Check with neighbors, building manager, household members
2. Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked delivered prematurely)
3. Contact us within 48 hours: support@techmartexpress.com
4. We'll investigate with carrier and file claim
5. Replacement or refund issued after investigation (3-5 business days)

### Damaged in Transit

If package arrives visibly damaged:
1. **Do not refuse delivery** - accept the package
2. Take photos of box and contents immediately
3. Keep all packaging materials
4. Contact us within 48 hours with photos
5. We'll arrange replacement or refund immediately

### Unauthorized Returns

Returns sent without authorization:
- May be refused or returned to sender
- Processing delayed by 2-3 weeks
- **Always get return authorization before shipping**

### Late Returns (After 30 Days)

Returns received after 30-day window:
- May be rejected and returned to sender
- Contact customer support if you have extenuating circumstances
- Manufacturer warranty may still apply for defective items
